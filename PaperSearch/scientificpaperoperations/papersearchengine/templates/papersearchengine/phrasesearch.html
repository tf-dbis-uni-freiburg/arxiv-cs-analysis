Phrase Search!

If you want to search for a phrase in the content of all papers, you have come to the right place! Sentences in which the word/phrase are found are displayed, along with the paper's metadata (authors, title, publication date, arXiV, and dblp URLs). You can also choose the number of results to be returned (max 1000). By default, 100 results are returned (if the search returns at least hundred results).
Example searches:  'semantic cognition',   'knowledge base completion'  and 'stochastic pooling'
